About Us

Vocational Training Institute for Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services of Ronald E. Presley (VTIREP)
Vision: In Pursuit of professional Excellence, Love, Truth and Liberty of mind
VTIREP: Is one of its kind and it is a place for excellence in the domain of mental wellness, healthy living, love and liberty of the mind in the acquisition of vocational/professional skills for greater possibilities and opportunities. We are the benchmark enforcers of the government’s initiative to promote Mental Health Care in Cameroon.

VTIREP is graced with a seasoned specialist in the person of the CEO with full-scale practice and experience in Mental Health Care acquired from studies in the USA and after having worked with the SAMHSA Project under the USA government.

Our curriculum is focused on evidence - based practices gathered from scientifically-based research and resources, and clinical protocols especially the TIMA and Boston psychiatric curriculum.
